    • Driver Drowsiness Management System

    The development of a comprehensive Driver Drowsiness Management System (DDMS) will employ cutting-edge technology, including computer vision, and machine learning algorithms, to continuously monitor the driver's facial features and behavior in real-time. It will detect signs of drowsiness or distraction, such as eyelid drooping, head nodding, or erratic steering, and issue timely alerts to the driver through visual and auditory cues. Furthermore, the DDMS will integrate with vehicle systems to activate safety measures like adaptive cruise control, lane departure warnings, and emergency braking when necessary. By prioritizing road safety, this system aims to reduce accidents caused by driver fatigue, potentially saving lives and minimizing the social and economic costs associated with road traffic accidents.
